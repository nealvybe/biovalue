# JnJ / IntraCellular Acquisition Analysis

## Deal Overview

**Asset:** Lumateperone (Caplyta)  
**Therapeutic Area:** CNS/Psychiatry  
**Indication:** Schizophrenia/Bipolar  
**Development Stage:** Approved (2019)  
**Year:** 2025

**Deal Structure:**
- Upfront Payment: $14,600M
- Total Deal Value: $14,600M

### Key Assumptions
- **Phase III Probability of Success:** 100%
- **NDA/Approval Probability:** 100%

## Valuation Analysis

The $14,600M upfront payment represents a **discount** to the base case rNPV of $14,850M. The upfront payment is $250M (2%) below the base case rNPV, representing an attractive valuation relative to standalone asset value.

### Deal Rationale

- **Commercial asset:** Approved product with established market presence and revenue visibility
- **Market potential:** CNS/Psychiatry therapeutic area with Schizophrenia/Bipolar indication

## Conclusion

The JnJ / IntraCellular acquisition demonstrates an attractive acquisition at favorable financial terms. With base case rNPV of $14,850M exceeding the $14,600M upfront, the deal offers immediate value creation potential.
