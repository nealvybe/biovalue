## Valuation Analysis

The $4,300M upfront payment represents a **premium** to the base case rNPV of $4,180M. The upfront payment exceeds the base case rNPV by $120M (3%), indicating the acquirer paid a strategic premium likely driven by portfolio fit, platform synergies, or competitive positioning.

### Deal Rationale

- **De-risked asset:** Pre-NDA status significantly reduces clinical and regulatory risk
- **Market potential:** Hepatology therapeutic area with Primary biliary cholangitis indication

## Conclusion

The Gilead / CymaBay acquisition demonstrates strong strategic value beyond standalone financial metrics. The 3% premium reflects confidence in the asset's commercial potential and strategic fit within the acquirer's portfolio.
