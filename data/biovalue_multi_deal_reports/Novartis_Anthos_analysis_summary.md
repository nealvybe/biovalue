# Novartis / Anthos Acquisition Analysis

## Deal Overview

**Asset:** Abelacimab (Factor XIa mAb)  
**Therapeutic Area:** Cardiovascular/Hematology  
**Indication:** Anticoagulation (NVAF, VTE)  
**Development Stage:** Phase III (3 trials)  
**Year:** 2025

**Deal Structure:**
- Upfront Payment: $925M
- Total Deal Value: $925M

## Valuation Analysis

The $925M upfront payment represents a **discount** to the base case rNPV of $4,498M. The upfront payment is $3,573M (386%) below the base case rNPV, representing an attractive valuation relative to standalone asset value.

### Deal Rationale

- **De-risked asset:** Phase III (3 trials) status significantly reduces clinical and regulatory risk
- **Market potential:** Cardiovascular/Hematology therapeutic area with Anticoagulation (NVAF, VTE) indication

## Conclusion

The Novartis / Anthos acquisition demonstrates an attractive acquisition at favorable financial terms. With base case rNPV of $4,498M exceeding the $925M upfront, the deal offers immediate value creation potential.
