The $2,700M upfront payment represents a **premium** to the base case rNPV of $2,450M. The upfront payment exceeds the base case rNPV by $250M (9%), indicating the acquirer paid a strategic premium likely driven by portfolio fit, platform synergies, or competitive positioning.

### Deal Rationale

- **Early-stage opportunity:** Phase I asset with significant upside potential if clinical development succeeds
- **Performance-based structure:** $1,000M in milestone payments align risk/reward
- **Market potential:** Metabolic/Obesity therapeutic area with Obesity indication

## Conclusion

The Roche / Carmot acquisition demonstrates strong strategic value beyond standalone financial metrics. The 9% premium reflects confidence in the asset's commercial potential and strategic fit within the acquirer's portfolio.
