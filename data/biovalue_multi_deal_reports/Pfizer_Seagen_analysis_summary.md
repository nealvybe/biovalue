# Pfizer / Seagen Acquisition Analysis

## Deal Overview

**Asset:** Enfortumab vedotin (Padcev)  
**Therapeutic Area:** Oncology/ADC  
**Indication:** Urothelial cancer  
**Development Stage:** Approved (2019)  
**Year:** 2023

**Deal Structure:**
- Upfront Payment: $43,000M
- Total Deal Value: $43,000M

## Valuation Analysis

The $43,000M upfront payment represents a **premium** to the base case rNPV of $41,200M. The upfront payment exceeds the base case rNPV by $1,800M (4%), indicating the acquirer paid a strategic premium likely driven by portfolio fit, platform synergies, or competitive positioning.

### Deal Rationale

- **Commercial asset:** Approved product with established market presence and revenue visibility
- **Market potential:** Oncology/ADC therapeutic area with Urothelial cancer indication

## Conclusion

The Pfizer / Seagen acquisition demonstrates strong strategic value beyond standalone financial metrics. The 4% premium reflects confidence in the asset's commercial potential and strategic fit within the acquirer's portfolio.
